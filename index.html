How We Work

Our Engineering Execution Process

We ensure systematic checkpoint controls at each phase from planning to handover.

01

Initial Consultation

Aligning project objectives, preliminary site checkups, and establishing ballpark estimates.

02

Planning & Feasibility

Drafting complete bill of quantities, scheduling timelines, and finalizing contract terms.

03

Architectural Design

Creating structural calculations, 3D renderings, and securing municipal clearance permits.

04

Active Construction

Foundation excavation, framework pouring, and masonry completion monitored daily.

05

Quality Inspection

Executing load tests, checking electrical grids, and validating safety parameters.

06

Project Handover

Final site cleanups, code compliance certificates handovers, and key distribution.
